An Archival Website of clips from an interview series that asked questions around, "Why do you love conversation?"

Naturally forming conversations around the topic that branch into various tangents and eventually drive back to the starting point. "Why do you love conversation?"

Structure

Each interview is categorised into various interrelated topics through which the website can be organised, reorganised and viewed in multiple ways.

Process

Each clip was printed, drawn on based on its categorization, scanned assembled into clips with audio and compiled into the archive.
